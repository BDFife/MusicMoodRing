Music Mood Ring

Music Mood Ring was originally built in about 24 hours at the hack event associated with the RE:Think Music Conference in Boston in April, 2012.

I wanted to see what would happen if I plugged the listening history of my friends into Rovi's album based mood/themes engine

Recently, I've added some styling to the site and migrated it to a heroku server.

Any questions or feedback, contact me on twitter @BDFife. You can learn more about me by visiting my site.
